FAREWELL TO 2018, REFLECTIONS FROM THATONEDANGUY

January 3, 2019

RBG Esports is incredibly excited to announce the signing of “Pewpew_af” and “GodFrey” to our roster. This week RBG Esports officially signed contracts with Alex “pewpew_af” Fernandez and John “GodFrey” Alarcon in the first steps towards creating a competitive PLAYERUNKNOWN’S BATTLEGROUNDS team. Pewpew will be serving as the official captain of RBG’s PUBG team, with more players and roster finalization to come in the near future.

“I can’t be more excited to be joining a great group of guys and girls who actually care about their players. RBG came out of nowhere and I immediately knew this is where I wanted to be. The future is bright.” -Pewpew on signing with RBG Esports

Pewpew has been consistently in the top 100 players on the PUBG solo fpp leaderboards, and RBG is looking forward to his performances as we finalize the squad. GodFrey brings a veteran’s experience to our roster with his past as both a professional Halo and Call of Duty player. Two more players and a dedicated sub will be added soon!

Stay tuned to our website and twitter (@RBG_Esports) for more updates as RBG makes its first foray into the competitive PUBG scene! In the meantime, feel free to drop into Pewpew’s twitch stream at https://www.twitch.tv/pewpew_af (tweet him @pewpew_af) or visit GodFrey’s stream at https://www.twitch.tv/godfreytv and welcome them to the RBG community!
