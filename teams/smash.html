I was born in the Bronx, New York on May 8th 2000. I’ve had a passion for gaming and content creating ever since I was young. My lifelong dream has been able to play on a stage and earn a living off gaming. I started to get really into esports when Call of Duty: Black Ops 3 came out for the Xbox 1. My team placed top 2 for the UMG Ladder, and I still go to my local Call of Duty tournaments being undefeated to this day. I got Into Rocket League about 2 years ago and been addicted to the game ever since. This has been my dream, to be scouted and play for an org that loves and cares for their players…. I think I’ve found it!
